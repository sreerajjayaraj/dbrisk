How to Use (Big, Simple)

You don’t need support. Everything runs on your machine. You control data and keys.

Step 1 — Pay & Download

  1. Click a plan above and complete payment on Stripe.
  2. After payment, you’ll be redirected to download the ZIP (or you’ll receive the link).
  3. Download and unzip the pack.

Step 2 — Unlock (if required)

Use the license key you received after payment.

  • Windows: powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File .\unlock.ps1 -LicenseKey YOUR_KEY
  • Linux: bash ./unlock.sh YOUR_KEY

Step 3 — Run the Collector

Choose your database and run the script. It collects read‑only metrics and produces local files.

  • Windows: UNLOCKED\Windows\<DB>\run.ps1
  • Linux: UNLOCKED/Linux/<DB>/run.sh

Outputs: output/snapshot.json and output/snapshot.redacted.json

Step 4 — Generate the Report (Client Controls OpenAI)

Set your OpenAI key and generate report.pdf locally.

  • Set key: OPENAI_API_KEY
  • Run:
    python3 UNLOCKED/openai_generate_report.py --snapshot output/snapshot.redacted.json --out_report output/report.json
    python3 UNLOCKED/report_to_pdf.py --report_json output/report.json --out_pdf output/report.pdf

Step 5 — Deliverables

  • output/report.pdf (board‑ready)
  • output/report.json (machine‑readable)
